Tipping Point

March 04, 2017

It felt great, just like the first time they cuddled, bodies close, his arms around her and peace. The same peace that kept her from leaving that first night.

He lift his head and whispered into her ear:
– How would you feel about having kids?

Oh no, it’s been two years, two wonderful if intense years together, he is wonderful, and stupid me hadn’t told him. Oh no.
– I am infertile – she said, as tears started running down her cheeks.
– Oh.

Just oh? Oh no, oh god no.

Just as she finished that thought, he slid her arms under her and turned her to face him.

His face, oh, his beautiful face.

Gently, he put his hand on her cheeks, brushed her tears away.
– How would you feel about having kids? - he repeated, finishing in a smile.

“I… Yes” - She closed her eyes as her mind drifted on how she would propose to him
